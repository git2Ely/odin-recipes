Ramen

Description

This classic ramen recipe brings together a rich broth, springy noodles, and a variety of flavorful toppings like soft-boiled eggs, sliced pork, and green onions. It's a comforting and satisfying dish that's perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken or pork broth
  • 2 packs of fresh or dried ramen noodles
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, sliced
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs
  • 4–6 slices of cooked pork belly or chicken
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels (optional)
  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed), cut into strips
  • Chopped green onions, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a pot, heat sesame oil over medium heat and sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the broth, soy sauce, and miso paste. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  3. Cook the ramen noodles in a separate pot according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. Taste the broth and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  5. Divide the cooked noodles between two bowls.
  6. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles.
  7. Top each bowl with slices of pork, half a soft-boiled egg, corn, nori, and green onions.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade ramen!
