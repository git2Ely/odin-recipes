Pad Thai

Description

This is an authentic pad thai recipe with rice noodles, chicken, peanuts, and stir-fried eggs in a traditional tamarind sauce. Garnish with fresh bean spouts and chives. This recipe was inspired by the pad thai at Thai Tom.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Eggs
  • Firm tofu
  • Shrimp or chicken (optional)
  • Bean sprouts
  • Green onions
  • Crushed peanuts
  • Lime wedges
  • Tamarind paste
  • Fish sauce
  • Palm sugar or brown sugar
  • Red chili flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water for about 30 minutes or until softened, then drain.
  2. In a small bowl, mix tamarind paste, fish sauce, and palm sugar to make the sauce. Set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and shallots, and stir-fry until fragrant.
  5. Add tofu and cook until golden brown. If using shrimp or chicken, add and cook until done.
  6. Push the ingredients to one side of the pan, then crack in the eggs and scramble until just set.
  7. Add the soaked noodles to the pan and pour the sauce over them. Toss everything to combine.
  8. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the noodles are tender and well-coated with the sauce.
  9. Add bean sprouts and green onions, and toss briefly to warm them through.
  10. Serve hot, garnished with crushed peanuts, lime wedges, and chili flakes if desired.
