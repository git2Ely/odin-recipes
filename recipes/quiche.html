Quiche

Description

This classic quiche recipe is a savory pie filled with a rich, creamy egg custard, cheese, and your choice of vegetables or meats. It's perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even dinner, and can be served warm or cold.

Ingredients

  • 1 9-inch pie crust (pre-baked or store-bought)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream or half-and-half
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (e.g., Gruyère, cheddar, or Swiss)
  • 1/2 cup cooked and crumbled bacon or sautéed spinach (optional)
  • 1/4 cup diced onions or shallots
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pinch of nutmeg (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. If not using a pre-baked crust, blind bake the pie crust for about 10 minutes and let it cool.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, cream, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  4. Spread the cheese evenly on the bottom of the pie crust.
  5. Add any optional fillings like bacon, spinach, or onions on top of the cheese.
  6. Pour the egg mixture over the fillings in the crust.
  7. Bake the quiche for 35–40 minutes, or until the center is set and the top is lightly golden.
  8. Allow to cool for at least 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
